About the ragdoll breed:

Ragdoll facts Fact 1: Crystal and Diamond are ragdolls and ragdolls are known for acting like dolls when you pick them up. Fact 2: Males weigh between 15-20 pounds and females weigh between 10-15 pounds. Fact 3: They take 2-4 years sometimes 5 years to fully grow to there mature size. Fact 4: They have a dog like personality and tend to follow there owners around and may play fetch or carry toys around. Visit this website for more amazing facts!
